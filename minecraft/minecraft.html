More Minecraft Screenshots

Here are a few more screenshots me and my friends collected while on our server over
winter break. We took pictures of any moments we could so we could remember our time spent
on here, since we knew that once classes started up again we wouldn't be able to play as much
as we had been.

Our servers we create and play on only last two or three months before we either get too
busy to play or just lose interest in the world, but it is still pretty nice to look back on
these screenshots to remember the times we had on one particular world together.
