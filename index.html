    • Minecraft with Friends

    One of the main things I did over winter break was spend time with my friends
    playing video games together. We play a lot of multiplayer games together throughout
    the semester, but we waited until winter break to start up our own Minecraft world.
    We spent pretty much the entire break working on our world, each of us having our
    own little spaces within a vast mountain range to build whatever we wanted.

    Spent Time with Family and Pets

    Despite spending a (probably) insane amount of Minecraft, I still spent a lot of
    time hanging out with my family and pets back at home in Northeast Philly. I got to
    see my mom, dad, sister, and my grandmother every day, which was pretty nice after
    not getting to see them as often as I had been over the course of the quarantine.
    Same thing goes for my pets! I have three cats at home, Patrick, Fat Boy, and Jinx,
    as well as my sister's dogs, Brody and Tully. I loved getting to see them every day.

    Sleep

    I really don't know what causes this, but pretty much every winter break so far I
    have become basically nocturnal. My sleep schedule gets so wild; I'll stay up all
    night, go to bed at 6am, sleep until 4pm, and the cycle repeats. I loved my unplanned
    midday naps.
